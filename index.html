Submit complaints in under 60 seconds with our intuitive form. Attach photos, select category, and set priority — all from your phone or laptop.

How CampusCare Works

Four simple steps to get your campus issue noticed and resolved.

1

Register & Login

Create your account with your college email and roll number. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2

Submit Complaint

Describe the issue, select category & location, and set priority level. Add a photo if needed.

3

Admin Reviews

Our admin team reviews the complaint, assigns it to the concerned department, and updates the status.

4

Issue Resolved

You get notified once resolved. You can rate the resolution and submit feedback for quality improvement.
